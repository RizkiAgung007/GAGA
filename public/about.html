About Us

The corporation has been in the food industry for more than 35 years, started its business in distribution and has become one of the biggest distributor in Indonesia In 1992, PT. Jakaran Tama with new identity started as a direct manufactur company that specialized in producing processed food products.

Realizing the importance of business and product diversifications, in the beginning of 1998, the company had invested 'GAGA' brand in our portofolio. In line with the increasing number of products varieties, the company has expanded its distribution scattered across all major cities in Indonesia.
